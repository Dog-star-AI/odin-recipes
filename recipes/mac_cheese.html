Mac And Cheese

Description:

Mac and cheese is a creamy, comforting dish made with tender pasta coated in a rich, cheesy sauce. Perfect as a main meal or side dish, it’s a timeless favorite for all ages.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni (or any short pasta)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1½ cups shredded cheddar cheese (or a mix of cheeses)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: breadcrumbs, parsley

Method:

  1. Cook the pasta: Boil the macaroni in salted water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the roux: In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until lightly golden.
  3. Add the milk: Gradually whisk in the milk to avoid lumps. Cook until the mixture thickens, about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Melt the cheese: Stir in the shredded cheese until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Combine: Add the cooked pasta to the cheese sauce and mix until evenly coated.
  6. Optional baking: Transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle with breadcrumbs, and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes for a crispy top.
