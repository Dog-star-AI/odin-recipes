Sandwich

Description:

This sandwich is a classic, satisfying combination of fresh and savory flavors. The crispy lettuce provides a refreshing crunch, while the juicy tomato slices add a burst of sweetness. The creamy mayonnaise (or butter) ties everything together, enhancing the rich, melty cheese and smoky ham or turkey. Encased in soft or lightly toasted bread, this sandwich is perfect for a quick lunch or snack, offering a balance of textures and tastes in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of your favorite cheese
  • 2 slices of ham or turkey (optional)
  • 2 leaves of lettuce
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise or butter

Method

  1. Prepare the bread: Spread mayonnaise or butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Layer the ingredients: On one slice of bread, stack the lettuce, tomato slices, cheese, and ham or turkey.
  3. Top it off: Place the second slice of bread on top, spread side down.
  4. Cut and serve:Cut the sandwich in half or quarters and enjoy!
